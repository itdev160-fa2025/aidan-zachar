The Unexpected Side Effects of Pens

The pen is an industry standard writing utensil, used for centuries.
However, researchers may have uncovered some unexpected side effects.

The Issue

While pens may seem harmless, they actually come with many previously unknown side effects as discovered by researchers.

"Pens have been found to be the leading cause of [unerasable] profanity emerging mysteriously on paper across our nation."

Researchers at Indiana University found and expressed in a press release earlier this week.

Furthermore, researchers at Black Mesa have found that pens used in scientific research led to a 23.29% increase in mistakes1.

What Are All of The Side Effects?

Below is a list of every side effect as found by the study performed by Indiana University.

  • Mysterious unerasable profane words and imagery
  • Increased writing mistakes
  • Stained sides of one's hand
  • Increased CO2 output due to frustrated sighs after a mistake is made.
